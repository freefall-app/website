Introducing: Freefall

The first non-profit dating app.

Modern dating apps are fundamentally flawed.

Imagine a system where the primary goal isn't to match compatible people but rather to keep users locked in an endless cycle of swipes. This is how dating apps currently operate.

Relationships are not just personal luxuries—they are necessities. Studies consistently show that good relationships are linked to longer lifespans, higher levels of happiness, reduced rates of violence, and more stable societies. In an age where over 50% of relationships begin online, it is imperative to address how those relationships are initiated.

Over half of all dating app users say their experience was “somewhat or very negative”.

These apps are engineered to maximize user engagement and subscription fees rather than ensuring quality matches. Users often report feeling like they're in an endless loop of fruitless swipes and superficial connections—a consequence of algorithms that prioritize quantity over quality, their own profits over their users’ needs.

Totally Free

Free from subscriptions and other fees, our focus remains on the user’s experience.

No Swiping

We eliminate the addictive and superficial nature of swiping.

Privacy First

We respect user privacy from the ground up.

Inclusivity

Our platform welcomes individuals from all walks of life, embracing diversity.

Freefall is a different type of dating app

As a not for profit corporation, Freefall is able to value the user expierence over all else.

Is Freefall really a non-profit?

Yes! Freefall is a California public benefit corporation. It is recognized by the IRS as a tax-exempt 501c(3) non-profit.

How do you make money?

We don't. Our services are supported by generous donors who recognize the importance of our work.

What can I do to help?

Yes, please tell your single friends to download the app. If you are an influencer or journalist, write about the app. If you code, join us on Github.

Can I donate?

Sure, in fact, we ask that our users consider donating to support our mission once they become engaged/married to someone they met through the app.

Aren't there more worthy causes you could be working on?

We feel that helping people find love is one of the most impactful things you can do. And the scientific literature backs it up.

How else is Freefall different from other dating apps?

Freefall is not your typical dating app. No swiping, no membership fees, no heights, and much more!

Launching soon

Freefall will be soft-launching in select locations during the Summer and launching at college campuses across the country in Fall.

Who We Are

Dylan saw firsthand how broken existing dating apps had become. He reached out to Brian, who had previously founded Nearby, and pitched him a radical idea for a new sort of dating app. While working at Nearby, Brian had seen how the pursuit of profit would derail the goal of helping users find compatible partners. Brian reached out to some of the people he had built Nearby with and Freefall was born. The team has continued to grow since then.
