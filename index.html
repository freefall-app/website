Freefall is the first dating app operated as a not for profit entity. That means our intrests are better aligned with yours.

Why a non-profit?

People have become tired of the status quo in dating apps: poor matches, high monthly prices, fake users. We argue that many of the things that have ruined dating apps are the result of a focus on user engagement in the pursuit of profit.

Allways free

Privacy First

We will never sell your data. Our incentives are aligned with yours.

Transparent

We don't have any secret matching algoithms. In fact, our code is open source and anyone can inspect it.
